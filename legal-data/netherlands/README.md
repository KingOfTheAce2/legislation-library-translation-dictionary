# Netherlands Legal Resources

This directory contains legal resources specific to **Netherlands law and jurisdiction**.

## Directory Structure

```
netherlands/
├── dictionaries/              # Legal dictionaries and glossaries (8 files)
├── legislation/
│   └── civil-procedure/      # Code of Civil Procedure (6 files, 8.5 MB)
└── reports/                   # Quality assurance (1 file)
```

## Content Overview

### Dictionaries (`dictionaries/`)

**8 files** containing legal terminology, glossaries, and translations:

1. **NL-legal-dictionary.json** (11 KB)
   - 495+ Dutch legal terms with Dutch definitions
   - Source: Raad van de Rechtspraak (Dutch Judiciary Council)
   - License: CC0 (Public Domain)

2. **NL-EN-legal-dictionary.json** (132 KB)
   - 800+ business and administrative legal terms
   - Source: business.gov.nl
   - License: CC0

3. **NL-EN-legal-glossary.json** (670 KB)
   - 1,800+ procedural terminology terms
   - Source: Civil Procedure experts (Burrough/Machon/Oranje/Frakes/Visser)
   - License: CC BY 4.0
   - SME reviewed

4. **NL-FR-legal-glossary.json** (4.5 KB)
   - Dutch-French legal terms
   - Source: Dutch/French government ministries

5. **NL-DE-legal-glossary.json** (4.4 KB)
   - Dutch-German legal terms

6. **NL-ES-legal-glossary.json** (4.3 KB)
   - Dutch-Spanish legal terms

7. **NL-EN-legislation-extracted-terms.json** (276 KB)
   - Auto-extracted terms from legislation

8. **NL-EN-legislation-glossary-additions.json** (23 KB)
   - Curated additions to glossary

### Legislation (`legislation/civil-procedure/`)

**6 files** containing the complete **Wetboek van Burgerlijke Rechtsvordering** (Code of Civil Procedure):

1. **NL-EN-civil-procedure-all.json** (2.3 MB)
   - Complete Code of Civil Procedure
   - 3,118 translation segments (paragraph-level)
   - Source: Official wetten.overheid.nl + professional translations
   - License: CC BY 4.0

2. **NL-EN-civil-procedure-book1.json** (1.5 MB)
   - Book 1 only (paragraph-level)

3. **NL-EN-civil-procedure-book2-3.json** (464 KB)
   - Books 2-3 (paragraph-level)

4. **NL-EN-civil-procedure-book4.json** (375 KB)
   - Book 4 (paragraph-level)

5. **NL-EN-example-sentences.json** (786 KB)
   - **Book 1 sentence-level segmentation**
   - 1,600+ paired Dutch-English sentences
   - Useful for linguistic analysis, translation memory, NLP
   - License: CC BY 4.0 / CC BY-NC 4.0

6. **NL-EN-civil-procedure-all-BACKUP.json** (2.3 MB)
   - Backup of complete civil procedure

### Reports (`reports/`)

1. **incomplete-translations-report.json** (62 KB)
   - QA report tracking translation completeness
   - Auto-generated by analysis scripts

## Legal System

**Jurisdiction:** Kingdom of the Netherlands (European territory)
**Legal System:** Dutch civil law (Continental European tradition)
**Primary Language:** Dutch (Nederlands, nl-NL)

**IMPORTANT:** This content is specifically for **Netherlands law as applied in the Netherlands**.

It does NOT cover:
- Belgian law (even though Belgian Dutch is similar)
- Suriname law
- Other Dutch-speaking regions

## File Naming Convention

All files follow the pattern: `[SOURCE-LANG]-[TARGET-LANG]-[CONTENT-TYPE].json`

Examples:
- `NL-EN-legal-dictionary.json` = Dutch → English legal dictionary
- `NL-legal-dictionary.json` = Dutch monolingual (NL-NL)
- `NL-FR-legal-glossary.json` = Dutch → French glossary

## Usage in Website

These files are loaded by:
- `index.html` - Main dictionary interface (via unified-dictionary.json)
- `legislation.html` - Civil Procedure browser (loads NL-EN-civil-procedure-all.json)
